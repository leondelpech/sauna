Our Mission is Simple

Build a Social Enterprise Sauna. Make it do good for its Community. Give it away to all.

  • Partner with local clubs to build resilient communities.
  • Return profits directly to community groups.
  • Create permanent public infrastructure for health and wellness.

A New Way of Doing Business

We believe community assets should be in the hands of the community. Our model is designed to make that a reality.

1. Built By Community

Through crowdfunding, Commun is built from its genesis by the very people who will use it. This tests demand, builds partnerships, and grows the good we can do from day one.

2. Reinvesting Profits

Instead of enriching shareholders, our profits are reinvested into local community groups, amplifying the wellbeing of the neighborhoods we serve.

3. The 5-Year Handover

After five years of operation, we hand over the entire asset—sauna, balance sheets, and all—to a community cooperative, creating permanent, self-sustaining infrastructure.
